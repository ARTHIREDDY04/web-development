JAIPUR

Jaipur, known as the Pink City, is a vibrant blend of history, culture, and modernity. It is famous for its majestic forts, palaces, and vibrant markets.

  2. Hawa Mahal
  3. City Palace
  4. Jantar Mantar
  5. Nahargarh Fort
